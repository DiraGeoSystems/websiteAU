Services

Dira GeoSystems provides training and consulting services for the Dira ProSuite product family and LOCATION FINDER. The DIRA PRO SUITE codebase is easily integrated into customer-specific solutions, allowing for cost-efficient and rapid development of your software projects. We are happy to share our vast experience in data production and cartography. Our special expertise covers the following topics:

  • Geodatabase modelling and design
  • Data and map production workflows, geodatabase versioning and (Oracle) database tuning
  • Designing workflows for the processing of very large datasets
  • Software development for ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Enterprise
